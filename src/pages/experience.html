My Working Experience

For the last 15 years I have been working in two different companies inside their CS / ICT divisons.My start was in the customer service area of a company and as a trainer for clients of their software product.After around 4 years training and knowing the product very well I internally retrained and moved into the software support division.

With 3 years of software support experience I moved to another company employed in the Product Development Team. The company is a division of Apotex pharmaceuticals specializing in SAAS for medication packaging.I am currently still employed as a Product Owner at this company: Click Here for their website 'myPak Solutions'.

Working in this position results in me having daily meetings and conversations with the programming & development team members. This is what inspires my interest in this area and so I have decided to formally be trained in both the front and backend development. Over the years I have learnt a number skills & used the following:

  • Databases: SQL + noSQL (Mongodb).
  • Remote access control systems: used Logmein, Screenconnect & Teamviewer.
  • Project management tools: mostly used JIRA.
  • Documentation: mostly used Confluence.
  • Version control systems: Used both GitHub & Bitbucket.
